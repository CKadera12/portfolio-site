FedWatch

Ludwig Von Mises Institute

Context

Mises Institute – an Austrian Economics nonprofit organization – created “FedWatch.” “FedWatch” is an online commentary series about the Federal Reserve and central banking. Mises Institute asked for a social media campaign to introduce “FedWatch” to current and hopefully new readers.

Solution

Many of the founding fathers – including those on the currency today – opposed fiat currency. I “defaced” the currency – using masks to portray the mysterious Federal Reserve. I believed this uncanny imagery could be understood by Mises supporters as well as attract new readers. The circle motif represents a spotlight.
